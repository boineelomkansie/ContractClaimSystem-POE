# Contract Monthly Claim System  
_A modern web-based system for managing lecturer monthly claims (ASP.NET Core MVC)._

---

## Overview  
The **Contract Monthly Claim System** streamlines the process of submitting, verifying, approving, and tracking lecturer claims.  
It automates calculations, improves transparency, and ensures accurate reporting for academic staff, programme coordinators, academic managers, and HR personnel.

## How to Use the System  

### **1. Submitting a Claim (Lecturer)**
1. Select lecturer name and contract  
2. Enter month, hours worked, and hourly rate  
3. Automatic total will calculate instantly  
4. Upload supporting documents  
5. Click **Submit Claim**

---

### **2. Verification (Programme Coordinator)**
1. Open the **Verify** tab  
2. Review the submitted claims  
3. Click **Verify** to confirm, or **Reject** to send back  

---

### **3. Approval (Academic Manager)**
1. Open the **Approve** tab  
2. Review all verified claims  
3. Approve or reject each one  

---

### **4. Tracking (Lecturer)**
Lecturers open the **Track Status** page to see where their claim is in the workflow.
